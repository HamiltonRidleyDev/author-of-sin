# Is God the Author of Sin? — Study & Production Materials

Working materials for a teaching video on the **sovereignty of God and the origin of evil** — a follow-up that revisits the question *"Is God the author of sin?"* years after a first attempt, to see what held up and what needed refining.

The heart of it isn't an argument to win. It's about three things you can't live without, and every one of them rests on this question:

- **how you walk through suffering** — whether it crushes you or holds you,
- **whether you can trust God** — even when, for a season, he looks like he is against you, and
- **whether you can have joy** — real joy — when life is hard and nothing makes sense.

The throughline: God **orders** creation (Genesis 1), sin **dis-orders** it, and God **re-orders** it toward redemption (Romans 8). God authors the whole story — including its darkest lines — the way an author writes, not the way a guilty man acts. We reason as far as Scripture goes, and worship where it ends.
